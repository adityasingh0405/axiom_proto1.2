## Understanding Big Data
### 5Vs Concept
- **Volume, Velocity, Variety, Veracity, and Value**: Concepts that classify any data as big data.
- Definition:
  - Volume: Large amounts of data (2.1 million snaps shared on Snapchat per minute).
  - Velocity: Rapid generation of data (188 million emails sent per minute).
  - Variety: Different types of data (search queries made on Google, videos watched on YouTube).
  - Veracity: Accuracy and reliability of the data.
  - Value: The usefulness and relevance of the data.
