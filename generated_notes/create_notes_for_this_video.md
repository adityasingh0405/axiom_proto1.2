# Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

## Key Concepts
- **Moore's Law** [11:34-11:44] - a video on how computers got so fast is recommended for further learning.
- **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** - introduced through an example of John Greenbot recognizing photos of the speaker.

## Detailed Explanation
John Greenbot is used as an example to illustrate AI in action. The program loaded into John Greenbot analyzes data from various photos, both of and not of the speaker. This data analysis enables John Greenbot to learn from the information. When presented with a new photo (a selfie of the speaker), John Greenbot attempts to classify it correctly.

## Important Points
- AI involves training models on data to enable them to perform specific tasks.
- The example demonstrates how AI can be applied in recognizing objects or patterns, such as faces in photos.
- There will be further discussion of AI and related concepts in **Episode 4** [02:04-02:42].

## Examples & Applications
- **Photo Recognition**: John Greenbot's ability to recognize the speaker from a selfie is an example of AI application in image recognition.
- **Data Analysis**: The program loads data from various photos to enable learning and future classification tasks.
